Otherworld

Otherworld is a Metroidvania style game centered around a robot who finds themselves rebooting on a battleground, injured and incapable of complete movement. Journey through a treacherous world collecting different body attachments, solving various puzzles, and exploring to find secrets that might aid you as you figure out what happened before you met your supposed end.

I used Photoshop for creating some of the sprites C# with Unity to develop the game. I used Git for version control and Atlassian Jira for project management to ensure proper team collaboration.

Some things I worked on include all of the menus and ability to select and deselect plugins, the level design, some of the animations, the energy system, and some of the environmental interactable objects. I also played around with the particle system to make item breaking effects and shader graph to add effects like glow to specific sprites and fog seen in the background. I also worked on the different layers of parallax used to make the backgrounds look interesting.

Game not available as web build, please head to the itch.io page to download.
