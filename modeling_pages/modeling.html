CAD and 3D Modeling

CAD and 3D Modeling using various tools and skills.
Tools: Autodesk Inventor, Cura, Photoshop

About

Here are some CAD and 3D modeling projects I have worked on to pick up skills outside of
just computer science. These skills allowed me to advance my embedded software projects
(3D printing structural parts).

ARC-170 Starfighter

A Lego Star Wars spaceship modeled first as individual pieces then combined in an assembly. This taught me the importance of measuring practices and constraints.

Conductivity Probe Model

This conductivity probedesign, after 3D printing, provided structure and
protection to components within my embedded software projects.

Helical Antenna Parts

This award winning helical antenna for Science Olympiad was created by
combining multiple 3D printed components. I modeled each component based
on measurements from helical antenna theory.

Baseplate Model

This baseplate, after 3D printing, held circuitry and Arduino microcontrollers
in place within my embedded software projects.
