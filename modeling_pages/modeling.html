CAD and 3D Modeling

CAD and 3D modeling using various tools and skills.
Tools: Autodesk Inventor, Cura, Photoshop

About

Here are some CAD and 3D modeling projects I have worked on to pick up skills outside of
just computer science. These skills proved helpful for my embedded software projects
(3D printing structural parts).

ARC-170 Starfighter

A Lego Star Wars spaceship modeled first as individual pieces then combined single assembly. Basically, built a LEGO car with modeled LEGO pieces.
in an assembly. This taught me the importance of measuring practices
and constraints.

Detector Probe Models

These designs, after 3D printing, provided structure and protection to
components within my embedded software projects.

Helical Antenna Parts

This model derived from Antenna Theory, after 3D printing, provided structure
to an award winning helical antenna for Science Olympiad.

Baseplate Models

These parts, after 3D printing, held circuitry and Arduino microcontrollers
I took in game reference pictures and used them to estimate dimensions and
in place within my embedded software projects.
